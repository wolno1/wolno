Synopsis

Wolno, a typical high school student approaching graduation, has his world flipped upside down when he crosses paths with Inu, a seemingly ordinary girl with an enigmatic aura. As Wolno delves deeper into her world, he discovers there's more to her than meets the eye. With graduation looming, Wolno realizes he has limited time to win her affection. Can he overcome the mysterious barriers before it's too late? Join Wolno and his friends as they navigate the complexities of teenage life, from friendships and romance to unexpected encounters and unforeseen challenges.
