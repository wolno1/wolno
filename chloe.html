Chloe

Chloe is a female cheetah and a character in Inu-sual Feelings. She's best friends with Laika and Inu and she's always with them. Known for her perfectionist tendencies and sophisticated demeanor, she consistently stays as the headclass every semester.

Physical appearance

She's got a sleek yellow coat with creamy patches running from her nose down to her chest, inner arms, and palms, continuing along her chest to her thighs, with her feet sporting a matching shade underneath. All of it's highlighted by a bold black stripe, and she's decked out in a scatter of black spots.

In her everyday wear, she prefers fancy outfits, often seen in a white dress with a light blue belt and cropped blazer. Her style revolves around light blue and white tones. Her hair is her pride, always maintaining a fluffy puff atop her head.

Personality

×

Wolno tarot card

Wolno appears to be the epitome of normalcy, blending seamlessly into the backdrop of any scene. He's unremarkable, average, and lacks any standout qualities. Some might even describe him as forgettable and flat, as he tends to keep to himself and doesn't actively engage in conversation.

Despite his unassuming nature, Wolno possesses a decent level of perceptiveness and intelligence. In typical social settings, he manages adequately, navigating interactions with ease. However, he harbors little enthusiasm for life's endeavors, often opting for minimal effort in his pursuits.

Everything changes when Wolno encounters Inu. In her presence, he transforms into a bundle of nerves, exhibiting anxiety and an inability to focus. This typically manifests as excessive rambling, stuttering, and difficulty maintaining his composure. Inu's mere presence disrupts Wolno's normally subdued demeanor, sending him into a spiral of self-consciousness and unease.

Roxanne

Wolno is one of Roxanne's best friends.

Despite the teasing and banter between them, they have a strong and close relationship. Roxanne often offers advice and guidance to Wolno, demonstrating her care for him. In return, Wolno values Roxanne's insights and relies on her as a confidant.

Wolno is like the funny one, compared to Roxanne. He relies on her for her wisdom and sensible advice when things get tough. Roxanne, on the other hand, is like the steady rock. She's always there to listen, give practical help, and offer comfort when Wolno needs it. Together, they make a great team, balancing each other out with Wolno's fun spirit and Roxanne's calm support.

Skye

Wolno and Skye are friends, mainly because she's Roxanne's other best friend.

Even when they are not as close to each other than as close to Roxanne, they have a strong bond and are close friends

Wolno sees Skye as some kind of Roxanne's extension, since she's always with her. Her presence often brings a more optimistic perspective to the group dynamic, complementing Roxanne's more grounded outlook. This balance of personalities enhances their friendship circle, creating a harmonious blend of perspectives and energies.

This dynamic trio demonstrates the beauty of diverse friendships and the strength that comes from embracing different perspectives within a relationship.

One of the first Wolno drawings

  • Wolno is loosely based on me from Highscool. I created him when I was in highschool, however, as I matured, Wolno's character gradually ceased evolving, making it challenging for me to fully embrace him as a mirror of my current self. While I entertain the idea of "updating" him to better align with my present identity, for the time being, he remains frozen in his original incarnation.
  • Wolno is my first OC (obviously), created around 2020.
  • I used to represent Wolno in a way girlier way, thing that wouldn't fit on the comic so I toned it down.
