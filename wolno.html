Wolno

Wolno is a male red fox and the central character in the comic Inu-sual Feelings. He shares a close friendship with Roxanne, alongside Skye and has a big crush on her classmate; Inu. Wolno is sa student in Tailspin Highschool and has a band with Roxanne and Skye (They are terrible).

Physical appearance

Wolno has soft, short fur and a remarkably long tail. His fur has a cream-colored pattern that extends from his inner thighs to his nose, as well as a brown pattern on his hands and paws from just below his elbows and knees. His pawpads are pink, and his eyes always have a relaxed, somewhat tired appearance. His eyes are purple, and they have a small triangular feature reminiscent of an old cartoon.

His common outfit consists on a red checkered shirt with straight dark blue jeans

Wolno's "performance" includes mustard-colored pants and an open, vintage-style jacket over a plain white shirt, which he pairs with his keytar; a Yamaha SHS-10.
