Physical appearance

Inu has long, black hair that falls down her back, and her face is bright, expressive and joyful. Her eyes are a bright honey color. She has a slender figure and stands at an average height.

Her fur is light yellow, with a creamy white countershade on her hands, feet, and front.

She typically wears a purple shirt paired with black pants.

Personality

×

Inu in the Inu branded outfit

Inu is a misterious person, she draws attention effortlessly wherever she goes, so effortlessly she's oblivious of her effects.

Inu seems to be kind and gentle, with soft spoken words and tender gestures she's like a sweet melody in a noisy world, enchanting all who cross her path.

Despite ticking all the boxes of a regular girl, Inu comes out as the object of admiration for many, Inu remains somewhat elusive, as if she's always just out of reach.
