Chloe

Chloe is a female cheetah and a character in Inu-sual Feelings. She's best friends with Laika and Inu and she's always with them. Known for her perfectionist tendencies and sophisticated demeanor, she consistently stays as the headclass every semester.

Physical appearance

She's got a sleek yellow coat with a creamy countershade running from her nose down to her chest, inner arms, and palms, continuing along her chest to her thighs, with her feet sporting a matching shade underneath. All of it's highlighted by a bold black stripe, and she's decked out in a scatter of black spots.

In her everyday wear, she prefers fancy outfits, often seen in a white dress with a light blue belt and cropped blazer. Her style revolves around light blue and white tones. Her hair is her pride, always maintaining a fluffy puff atop her head.

Personality

×

Chloe watercolor art

Intelligent, proud, refined, perfectionist, and elegant—these core qualities define Chloe's essence perfectly.

She treats everyone with respect, consistently maintaining her composure and commanding presence wherever she goes. Chloe has a strong preference for things being done her way.

She is particularly protective of her friends, Laika and especially Inu, whom she often assists due to Inu's constant unwanted company.

Chloe's speech is typically formal, characterized by sophisticated and elegant language.

Relationships

Inu

Inu and Chloe are tight pals, forming two-thirds of a trio with Laika.

Chloe, the trio's leader, deeply values her friendship with Inu. She's fiercely protective of Inu, shielding her from anyone who might exploit her kindness and innocence.

Chloe is not only Inu's best friend but also her closest confidante. To Chloe, Inu is more than just a friend; she's like a little sister to watch over and care for, that's why, if you see Inu, you'll surely find Chloe nearby.

Laika

Laika is the last member of the best friends trio. Similar to Chloe and Inu, Laika is always close to Chloe but takes on a more supportive and less assertive role. Wherever Chloe goes, Laika follows, though she often goes unnoticed by others. Laika deeply admires and respects Chloe, who, in turn, sees Laika as someone to protect and fully trust.

Roxanne

Roxanne and Chloe seem to have a shared history, though the details are unclear. Roxanne clearly doesn't like Chloe, but she seems to know her very well.
