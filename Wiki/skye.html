Skye

Skye is a female snake and a character in Inu-sual Feelings. She's best friends with Wolno and Roxanne, being his voice of reason and sometimes the opposite.

Physical appearance

Skye is a long, elegant blue snake with a light blue countershade. She enhances her appearance with magenta eyeshadow, which beautifully complements her black eyes.

Personality

Roxane doodles

Skye is a good noodle. She is a kind and sweet girl with a gentle personality that perfectly balances Roxanne's rough demeanor. Friendly, noble, innocent, and kind-hearted, she embodies idealism.

Relationships

Roxanne

Skye and Roxanne compliment themselves so well you could count them as one person

At first, they can be very different but that's what keeps them together, the input from one is always useful for the other

Most of the time, you can find Skye traveling on Roxanne's back or her backpack, since it's harder for her to keep up with a normal walking speed.

Wolno

Wolno and Skye are friends, mainly because she's Roxanne's other best friend.

Even when they are not as close to each other than as close to Roxanne, they have a strong bond and are close friends

Wolno sees Skye as some kind of Roxanne's extension, since she's always with her. Her presence often brings a more optimistic perspective to the group dynamic, complementing Roxanne's more grounded outlook. This balance of personalities enhances their friendship circle, creating a harmonious blend of perspectives and energies.

Trivia

  • Given the rarity of snakes in her area, finding appropriate clothes is a challenge. She often wears garments she makes herself or finds creative alternatives. Her usual outfit consists of a regular shirt paired with a customized leg warmer worn as a vest. Fortunately, her choker and hat fit perfectly, despite her being a snake.
