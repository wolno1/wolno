Laika

Laika is a female dog and a character in Inu-sual Feelings. She's best friends with Inu and mainly Chloe always following her around. She is obsessed with space and sci-fi, often too distracted by them.

Physical appearance

Laika is a slender dog with a distinctly wild appearance, characterized by a long snout and ears. Her fur is predominantly white with dark markings that extend over her eyes, head, shoulders, back, top of tail, and thighs. Her paw pads are pink with black spots, adding to her unique features.

Personality

×

Laika yapping

Laika is a happy girl who often goes unnoticed by others, but she doesn't mind because she finds contentment in Chloe's company. She's intelligent and has the potential to excel academically if she could divert her focus from her fascination with sci-fi and space.

Relationships

Chloe

Laika is the last member of the best friends trio. Similar to Chloe and Inu, Laika is always close to Chloe but takes on a more supportive and less assertive role. Wherever Chloe goes, Laika follows, though she often goes unnoticed by others. Laika deeply admires and respects Chloe, who, in turn, sees Laika as someone to protect and fully trust.
