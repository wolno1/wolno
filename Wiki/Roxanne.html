Roxanne

Roxanne is a female lizard creature and a character in Inu-sual Feelings. She's best friends with Skye and Wolno, being his voice of reason and sometimes the opposite.

Physical appearance

Roxanne, a scalie, boasts a sleek appearance with smooth, hairless skin (except for her eyelashes, for some reason) Her skin is a soft, pink hue accented by a striking cyan color on her jaw, chest, soles, and palms, creating a vivid counter shade. Her eyes shine in bright magenta, contrasting against yellow sclera. Completing her distinctive look, she sports black claws on her hands and feet, two prominent horns adorning her forehead, and a set of sharp teeth.

Personality

×

Roxane doodles

Roxanne is an active girl who likes to express her feelings and thoughts openly, often with a touch of sarcasm and pessimism. While she might sometimes come across as having anger issues, she is a nice person at heart. She generally keeps to herself, preferring not to talk to others unless it's necessary or if she's with Wolno and Skye.

Relationships

Wolno

Wolno is one of Roxanne's best friends.

Despite the teasing and banter between them, they have a strong and close relationship. Roxanne often offers advice and guidance to Wolno, demonstrating her care for him. In return, Wolno values Roxanne's insights and relies on her as a confidant.

Wolno is like the funny one, compared to Roxanne. He relies on her for her wisdom and sensible advice when things get tough. Roxanne, on the other hand, is like the steady rock. She's always there to listen, give practical help, and offer comfort when Wolno needs it. Together, they make a great team, balancing each other out with Wolno's fun spirit and Roxanne's calm support.
