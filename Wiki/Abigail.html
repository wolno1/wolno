Physical appearance

Abigail is a slim white bunny with very dark hair and red eyes.

She rocks a great collection of piercings on her ears and a piercing on her bellybutton, alongside black makeup (mascara, lipstick and rimmel)

Personality

×

Abigail at her first day in work

Abigail is very passionate about art, which makes her sad because being an artist is a perpetual struggle, which lead her to work at the McThingies restaurant support her art career.

She is kind with the clients, a good worker and a good student, but all the pressure of this, her family and her relationships end up making her feel numb and depersonified
