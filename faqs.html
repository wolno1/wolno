Can I repost/use your art?

Of course, as long you give me proper credits (No "Credits to the artist", I would prefer a link to one of my social networks) you can use my art as any way you want unless you use it for profit or to promote something. If you are reposting, DO NOT remove my sign or edit my work.

About the rating of my art

I consider myself a SFW account, but my artwork can get to be suggestive or include suggestive themes on my art, so I would suggest following me if you are at least 16 years old.

Can I use your characters?

As long as it is SFW, yes, if you do, please tag me or send it to me, I would love to see and support!

If it is NSFW, I would like you to ask first, sadly I won't be able to RT or interact since my account is SFW.
